The Problem

School commute in India
is broken.

Finding a safe van driver today means standing outside the school gate or asking other parents. There's no platform, no verification, no tracking — just hope.

🚌
School buses take forever

With 40+ kids on a single route, children spend 60–90 minutes in a bus after school. That's hours of their day wasted every week.

❓
No way to find drivers

Parents resort to standing outside school gates or relying on word-of-mouth. There's no marketplace, no directory, no standard process.

⚠️
Unregulated & unsafe

Most private vans are overcrowded, unverified, and never inspected. No background check on the driver. No fitness certificate on the vehicle.

👁️
Zero visibility for parents

Once your child is in the van, you have no idea where they are, when they boarded, or when they'll arrive. No tracking. No alerts. No confirmation.

The Anzen Driver Standard

No driver joins Anzen without completing our full verification pipeline. We check what matters most — and we check it again, every 6 months.

  • Aadhaar-based identity verification (OKYC)
  • Driving License validation via government database
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC) check
  • Police Clearance Certificate — no criminal record
  • Vehicle Fitness Certificate — safety compliant
  • Capacity enforcement — no overcrowding, ever
  • Ongoing rating system — parents review after every month
  • Automatic suspension if re-verification fails
📵
Number Masking

Your phone number and the driver's are never shared directly. All calls go through Anzen's masked virtual numbers.

🔐
Child Data Privacy

Fully compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023. Your child's data belongs to you — delete it anytime, and it's gone everywhere.

🔄
6-Month Re-Verification

Driver credentials don't expire quietly. Every 6 months, full reverification runs automatically. Parents are notified if anything changes.
